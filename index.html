Description of the course

What is this course about?

This course is designed to equip members of the Theme Park & Toy Engineers (TPTE) and aspiring leaders with the essential "soft skills" required to excel in the themed entertainment and toy industries. While technical skill is the "what" of your career, professionalism and leadership constitute the "how" and the "why." Professionalism isn't just about wearing a suit or showing up on time; it’s a standard of conduct. It encompasses accountability, respect, and ethical decision-making. Leadership is the ability to guide, inspire, and coordinate people toward a common goal. It’s not reserved for people with "Manager" in their title; it’s an attitude.
